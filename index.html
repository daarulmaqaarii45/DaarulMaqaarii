Daarul Maqaaril Litahfeedhil Qur'aan wa Ulumil Arabiyyah is a Tahfeedh and Arabic learning institution located at 180 Bamgbose Street, Lagos Island, dedicated to raising huffaadh grounded in sound Tajweed, Aqeedah, Fiqh, and classical Arabic.

Our programme runs from Nursery through Ibtidaiyyah, dedicated Tahfiz tracks, and adult classes — built on disciplined daily memorization, qualified teachers, and a structured curriculum spanning the Islamic sciences.
